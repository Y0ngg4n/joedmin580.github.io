Do you wanna be programmer too?
Last update: January 4th 2021

For beginner programmers could be good to watch some tutorials, for example on YouTube the freeCodeCamp.org channel or their website, or to look on w3schools.com which has a lot of tutorials, exercises and quizzes on several programming languages. The next thing, that could help is to read some books, for example Think Like a Programmer written by V. Anton Spraul or the Clean Code written by Robert C. Martin. All are available in English and maybe in other languages. Both are on my Google drive as a PDF files.
